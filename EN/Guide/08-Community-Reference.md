# 08. Community Reference

## Goal

Absorb high-signal community material without losing the official baseline.

## Recommended sources

- `UberGuidoZ/Flipper`
- `UberGuidoZ/Flipper-IRDB`
- `jamisonderek/flipper-zero-tutorials`
- `awesome-flipperzero`
- `DroomOne/Flipper-Plugin-Tutorial`
- `flipperzero-esp-flasher`
- `FlipperHTTP`
- `flipwire`
- `flipperzero-gps`
- `flipperzero-qrcode`

## Usage rules

- use community sources to supplement examples, tools, and workflow experience
- do not treat community firmware behavior as official platform behavior
- do not treat one successful project as a universal platform rule

## Expected outcome

- decide which community sources belong in the knowledge base
- separate official facts from community experience
