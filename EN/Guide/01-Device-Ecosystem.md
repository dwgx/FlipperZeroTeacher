# 01. Device and Ecosystem

## Goal

Build a complete understanding of the device interface, desktop tools, mobile tools, and day-to-day management flow.

## Core pages

- `https://docs.flipper.net/basics/desktop`
- `https://docs.flipper.net/qflipper`
- `https://docs.flipper.net/mobile-app`
- `https://docs.flipper.net/apps`
- `https://docs.flipper.net/zero/development/cli`

## What to understand

- the relationship between Desktop, status bar, main menu, Archive, and Device Info
- the role of `qFlipper` in update, backup, restore, and file management
- the role of the Mobile App in BLE updates, sync, and app installation
- the relationship between Apps and Flipper Lab
- the basic value of CLI, logs, and subsystem shells

## Expected outcome

- manage files and backups correctly
- maintain the device through desktop and mobile workflows
- know where to find logs and command-line access
